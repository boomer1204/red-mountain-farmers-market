About

The Red Mountain Farmers Market is out in the East Mesa community to bring fresh local food and small business to its community. We have partnered up with Zaharis Elementary School to make this all possible. Proceeds from the market will be set aside to allow the school to improve the learning environment for its students. None of this could be possible without the great support of Zaharis and its amazing staff. The market will have a great variety of fresh produce as well as a variety of great artisan vendors and food to eat. Come out and support small local businesses and have a great Saturday morning.
